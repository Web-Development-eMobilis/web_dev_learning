Wildlife Conservation Measures

  1. Trash isn’t just ugly, it’s harmful. Birds can trap their heads in plastic rings. Fish can get stuck in nets. Plus, trash pollutes everyone’s natural resources. Do your part by putting trash in its place and keeping your community clean.
  2. Find new ways to use things you already own. Manoh Philip Sesay, a YALI Network member in Sierra Leone, uses discarded plastics to create shopping bags, handbags and computer bags.
